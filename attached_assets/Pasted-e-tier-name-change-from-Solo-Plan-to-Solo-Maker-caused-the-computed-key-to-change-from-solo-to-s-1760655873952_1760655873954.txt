This new key was unrecognized by:

subscription_tiers.json
app/utils/permissions.py's TIER_ORDER
Consequently, billing and permission checks treated the tier as "unknown_tier", leading to the "invalid tier" error.

Recommended solutions:

Rename the tier back to "Solo Plan".
Update configurations to recognize solo_maker.
Implement a persistent key column in SubscriptionTier to prevent future regressions.
